CAST CHARACTERS ABOUT HOME

TORI VEGA

Tori Vega is the protagonist of the hit Nickelodeon show, Victorious.Tori Vega is the younger sister of Trina Vega who attends at Hollywood Arts, a performing arts high school.She experiences the biggest change of her life during the Big Showcase when she transfers from Sherwood High School to Hollywood Arts . This enables her to learn more about acting, singing, and dancing and make new friends and frenemies.

ANDRE HARRIS

Andre Harri is Tori's best friend. Andre is an aspiring musician who loves to perform and write his songs. He can play many instruments, including the guitar, keyboard, piano, and the French horn, but prefers the keyboard and piano. Andre takes his music seriously and is usually seen playing at all of Tori's performances. He is a talented singer and songwriter, having co-written nearly every song Tori has sung. He also often gets Tori to sing back-up for him at his performances.

ROBBIE SHAPIRO

Robbie Shapiro is one of Tori's friends at Hollywood Arts. He is a ventriloquist who almost always carries his best friend Rex with him. Robbie is considered extremely nerdy, shy, and socially awkward, but a good, loyal, and honest to a friend and others. Robbie has a great deal of trouble with relationships and attracting girls.
